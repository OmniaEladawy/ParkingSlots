How it Works

Three Steps to Park

Look

Choose the area you are in and find the parking spots near your destination.

Check

The system check whether there is a parking space available in the area or not.

Park

If there is a space available the application will show the number of the spot and location.

How The App Works

  • Dawnload the App from Google Play.
  • Login/signUp for the system through your mobile application.
  • Choose the area you are in.
  • The system check whether there is a parking spot available in the area or not.
  • If there is a spot available the app will show the number of the spot and location and if not the system will notify you that there isn’t spot available.

ParkingSlots free app gives you a smarter way to park when you're on the go.
